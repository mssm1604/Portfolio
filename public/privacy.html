This policy describes how Santiago Monsalve ("I", "me", or "the operator") collects, uses, and protects information in connection with my personal portfolio website at https://santiagomonsalve.com and any applications, integrations, or projects I develop and operate, including applications that connect to the Meta Platform (collectively, the "Services").

I take privacy seriously. This document is written to be read — not skimmed past. If anything here is unclear, please reach out at smonsalve386@gmail.com.

Scope

This policy applies to:

  • Visitors to my portfolio website at https://santiagomonsalve.com.
  • Operators and administrators of Facebook Pages who install or authorize any Meta Platform application I develop.
  • End users whose public Facebook content (such as comments) may be processed by such applications.

Information I Collect

From the portfolio website

The website is a static portfolio. I do not run analytics tracking, advertising pixels, or behavioral cookies. Standard server logs (IP address, user agent, timestamp, requested URL) may be retained briefly by the hosting provider for security and operational purposes.

From Meta Platform applications

When a Page admin authorizes one of my applications via Facebook Login, the application may access — strictly within the permissions granted — the following:

  • Page metadata: page name, page ID, and the granting user's role on the page.
  • Page Access Tokens: short-lived or long-lived tokens issued by Meta, used to perform authorized actions on behalf of the page.
  • Public Page content: posts published by the page and public comments left on those posts, including the commenter's display name and Facebook user ID.
  • Webhook event payloads: real-time notifications of comment activity delivered by Meta when subscribed.
Note I do not collect private messages, personal contact information, friend lists, photos beyond what is publicly attached to a page post, or any information beyond what is necessary for the application's stated purpose.

How I Use Information

Information collected through Meta Platform applications is used solely to operate the application's stated functionality, which may include:

  • Receiving comment notifications via webhook and parsing them.
  • Classifying comment sentiment using third-party AI services (see Third Parties below).
  • Drafting suggested replies for human review and approval.
  • Posting human-approved replies back to the originating comment thread.
  • Maintaining operational logs for debugging and audit purposes.

I do not use this information for advertising, profiling, model training, profile-building of individual commenters, or any purpose unrelated to the application's authorized function.

Legal Basis

Where applicable (e.g., for users in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, or other jurisdictions with comparable laws), processing is based on:

  • Consent: Page admins explicitly grant permissions during the OAuth authorization flow.
  • Legitimate interest: operating the requested service in a secure and reliable manner.
  • Contractual necessity: where applicable, fulfilling the service the page admin has chosen to enable.

Data Retention

I retain data only as long as necessary for the application's stated purpose:

  • Webhook payloads and execution logs: typically retained for up to 30 days for debugging, then deleted automatically.
  • Operational logs in spreadsheets: retained at the page admin's discretion; admins may delete entries at any time.
  • Page Access Tokens: retained only while the page admin's authorization remains active. Upon revocation, tokens are deleted.
  • Server logs: retained briefly per the hosting provider's standard retention.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Request access to information I hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your information.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing.
  • Request data portability where technically feasible.
  • Withdraw consent at any time (without affecting prior lawful processing).
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

To exercise any of these rights, contact me at smonsalve386@gmail.com. I will respond within 30 days.

Page admins: revoking access

You can revoke an application's access to your Facebook Page at any time through Facebook's Business Integrations settings (Settings → Business Integrations → Remove). Once revoked, the application can no longer access your page data.

Commenters: removing a comment from processing

If you would like a specific comment removed from any operational log I maintain, contact me with the comment URL or ID and I will delete it.

Data Sharing

I do not sell, rent, or trade information. I share information only with the third-party processors listed above, strictly to operate the Service. I will disclose information if required by law, valid legal process, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of the operator, users, or the public.

Security

I take reasonable measures to protect data, including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit.
  • Access tokens stored as encrypted credentials within the workflow platform, not in plaintext.
  • Principle-of-least-privilege scoping for all OAuth permissions requested.
  • Prompt revocation upon admin request.

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but I monitor for and respond to incidents promptly.

International Transfers

Data may be processed in jurisdictions other than your own (e.g., the United States or the European Union), as my providers operate globally. Where required, I rely on Standard Contractual Clauses or equivalent safeguards offered by these providers.

Children's Privacy

The Services are not directed at children under 13 (or 16 in some jurisdictions), and I do not knowingly collect information from them. If you believe a child has provided information through one of my applications, contact me and I will delete it.

Meta Platform Compliance

Applications I develop for the Meta Platform comply with Meta's Platform Terms and Developer Policies. Specifically:

  • I request only the minimum permissions required for the stated functionality.
  • I do not transfer Platform Data to ad networks, data brokers, or any prohibited recipients.
  • I delete Platform Data upon revocation of access or upon a valid deletion request.
  • I do not use Platform Data to discriminate or to make eligibility decisions.
